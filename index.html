Introduction To Tally


Introduction: Tally.ERP 9 is a comprehensive business management software which can be used to record all of your business's financial transactions, including sales, purchases, expenses, and income. It also provides reports that can help you track your financial performance and identify areas where you can improve. Tally.ERP 9 educational version is a free version of the software that can be used by students and educational institutions for learning purposes. It has some limitations compared to the paid version, but it can still be used to learn the basics of accounting and business management.

Usage Range or Fields

Manufacturing: Tally can be used to track inventory, manage production, and generate reports on financial performance.
Retail: Tally can be used to track sales, manage inventory, and generate reports on customer behavior.
Service: Tally can be used to track time and expenses, manage customer relationships, and generate reports on project profitability.
Construction: Tally can be used to track materials, manage labor, and generate reports on project costs.
Education: Tally can be used to track student records, manage finances, and generate reports on academic performance.

The company info menu

The Company info menu is used to Create, Shut, Select, Rename or Delete a Company. It is also used to change vault password, Security Control etc. You can bring the Company info menu by pressing ALT+F3 on the keyboard

Q1. How to create a company?

Press ALT+F3. Company info menu will appear.

Go to CREATE COMPANY

Fill up all necessary fields such as Name, Address, Contact details, financial year, password etc.

Press CTRL+A [ All Accept ]

Q2. How to rename a company

Press ALT+F3. Company info menu will appear.

Go to ALTER

Change the field details you want such as Name, Address, Contact details etc.

Press CTRL+A [ All Accept ]

Q4. How to select a company that has been shut already?

Press ALT+F3. Company info menu will appear.

Go to Select Option

Search for the company

Choose the company and then hit ENTER.

Q5. How to delete a company?

Press ALT+F3. Company info menu will appear. Go to ALTER Option Hit

Enter button Press ALT+D. Then press enter to agree the dialogue to the

box.

Go to Change Tally Vault

Option Create a new password and repeat it. Then accept it.

Q7. How to add stock items?

On the Gateway of Tally menu, go to Inventory info.

Go to Stock Item Option

Create a Stock item such as Potato, salt etc.

On the Unit field Press ALT+C and create an unit like Kg, Ltr, Pcs etc. and accept it.

Fill the Quantity and rate as per stock item. Then accept it by pressing CTRL+A

How to see the stock items?

Go to ‘ Stock Summery ’ in gateway of Tally Menu

Q9. How to add Capital Amount?

On the Gateway of Tally menu, go to Accounts info.

Go to Ledgers Option

Create a Ledger called My Capital under Capital AC.

CTRL+A to accept it.

On the Gateway of Tally, go to Accounting Vouchers. Press F6 for Receipt Voucher

Press F2 and enter the date. Then on the particulars Select the My Capital Ledger then Enter

Enter an amount in the credit field and then hit Enter

Now select the Cash account. Then Accept it.

Q10. How to transfer amount from cash to bank?

To transfer amount from cash to bank, you must have a bank ledger.

Go to Accounting voucher in Gateway of tally menu

Press F4 for Contra.

Enter the desired date and then select Cash Ledger in the particulars and enter an amount.

Select the Bank ledger to which you want to transfer the amount

Press CTRL+A to accept the voucher.

Your amount has been transferred from Cash to Bank successfully.

Q11. How to make purchase entry in tally?

Go to Accounts info in Gateway of tally menu and then go to Ledgers.

Create a Purchase Ledger which is under Purchase Account.

Create a ledger for the creditor party [Sundry Creditor].

Now go to Accounting voucher and Press F9 and then enter the date. Then select the creditor party [Sundry Creditor] and Purchase ledger.

On the particulars, select an item or create one by pressing ALT+C then enter the Qty. and rate then accept the voucher

Q12. How to make payment against the purchase entry?

Go to Accounting Voucher and then press F5 [Payment Voucher] and enter the date.

On the particulars, select the party whom you want to pay.

Now select the ledger from which you want to pay. For example Cash, IDBI G-Pay etc.

Press Ctrl+A to accept the entry.

Q13. How to make sales entry in tally?

Go to Accounts info in Gateway of tally menu and then go to Ledgers.

Create a Sales Ledger which is under Sales Account.

Create a ledger for the creditor part [Sundry Debtors].

Now go to Accounting voucher and Press F8 and then enter the date. Then select the Debtor party [Sundry Debtor] and sales ledger.

On the particulars, select an item or create one by pressing ALT+C then enter the Qty. and rate then accept the voucher

Q14. How to receive the amount against the sales entry?

Go to Accounting Voucher and then press F6 [Receipt Voucher] and enter the date.

On the particulars, select the party from whom you want to get the amount.

Select the ledger via which you want receive the amount. For example Cash, IDBI G-Pay etc.

Press Ctrl+A to accept the entry.
